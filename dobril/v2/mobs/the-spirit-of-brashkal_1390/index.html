The spirit of Brashkal

The spirit of Brashkal is an unknown level mob encountered in Daemon's Ground, Graveyard.

name
The spirit of Brashkal
description
Brashkal was a templar of long ago, he was not greatly reknowned but his skill with a whip was uncommon among his choice of profession. He had killed many in his life for the name of his god, maybe that is why in his afterlife he is still here, guarding his tomb "for his god" and is forced to carry around the weight of his final belongings. He would not think twice about killing you. He wouldn't think at all, actually. The spirit of Brashkal's body appears to be translucent.
