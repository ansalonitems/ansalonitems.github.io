A resentful skeleton fighter is an unknown level mob encountered in Daemon's Ground, Graveyard.

name
A resentful skeleton fighter
description
Pure evil eminates from the soulless form in front of you, it knows only one thing -- hate, and it lives its passions as best it can. It was once a human of course, or maybe an elf or half-elf, but now is no more than a bunch of dusty bones reanimated by an unknown source to dispose of the living. Some of its old armor still adorns its skeletal form in a decadent display of history.
