appraisal

#Description

Appraise is a skill that when used on items, will give you an estimate of their values when sold and sacrificed.

#Example output

You believe a Modified deepsea fishing pole is worth about 14986 copper coins.
You think you'd get 3 copper for sacrificing a Modified deepsea fishing pole to Solinari.

#Location

Appraisal is a found skill acquired in The City of Neraka.

#Steps

You can acquire appraisal by visiting The Jewelry Shop in The City of Neraka.

  1. Saragurb, the shopkeeper, will offer to appraise things for you
  2. say appraise
  3. Saragurb will offer to train you in appraisal for 50 gold
  4. give 50 gold saragurb
  5. Saragurb will give you the appraisal skill
