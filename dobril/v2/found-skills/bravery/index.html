Dobril

bravery

#Description

Bravery is a skill that increases resistance to fear effects.

#Example output

You channel your bravery against the overwhelming fear!

#Location

Bravery is a found skill acquired in Thelgaard Keep.

#Steps

You can acquire bravery by visiting A knight of the crown inThelgaard Keep.

  1. study knight
  2. The knight will give you a notebook and tell you to take notes about him then take them to his officer
  3. jot notes
  4. Go to Kendon, Knight of the Rose
  5. give note kendon
  6. say review
  7. Kendon will sign off on the notes then tell you to take them to the Solamnic guide
  8. Go to A Solamnic guide
  9. give study guide
  10. The guide will give you the bravery skill
