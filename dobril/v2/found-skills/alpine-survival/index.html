alpine survival

#Description

Alpine survival is a skill that reduces the movement penalty for walking in mountainous or icy terrain and allows you to gather food there.

#Example output

You begin to gather food.
You look around for needed items.
You pick a few bits and pieces.
You gather enough to food to make a acceptable meal.

#Location

Alpine survival is a found skill acquired in Sentinel Peaks.

#Steps

You can acquire alpine survival by visiting A rock climber inSentinel Peaks.

  1. The climber will greet you
  2. wave
  3. The climber will ask if you' a climber too
  4. say no
  5. The climber will give you a book to study
  6. study book
  7. The climber's book will give you the alpine survival skill
