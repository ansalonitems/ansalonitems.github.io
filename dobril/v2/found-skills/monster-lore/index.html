monster lore

#Description

Monster lore is a skill that occasionally increases your chances to hit non-humanoid enemies.

#Example output

Your knowledge of song bird-kind provides you guidance!
Your slime === OBLITERATES === a tiny sparrow! (97)

#Location

Monster lore is a found skill acquired in Palanthas.

#Steps

Monster lore is a found skill acquired by studying a specific book in the Library of Palanthas.

  1. Head to The Biology Section in the Library
  2. study book
  3. The book will give you the monster lore skill
