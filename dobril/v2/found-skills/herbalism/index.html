herbalism

#Description

Herbalism is a skill that lets you gather herbs for mulitple purposes.

#Example output

You begin to gather herbs for healing.
You pick a few bits and pieces.
You pick a few bits and pieces.
You gather enough to create a bunch of healing herbs.

#Location

Herbalism is a found skill acquired in The village of Ash.

#Steps

You can acquire herbalism by visiting An elven druid in The village of Ash.

  1. An elven druid will ask if you're looking for something
  2. say yes
  3. An elven druid will ask if you want him to give you something or teach you something
  4. say teach
  5. An elven druid will give you the herbalism skill
